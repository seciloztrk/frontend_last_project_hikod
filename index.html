The place of women in the IT industry

The situation of women in the technology sector remains one of the pending challenges in terms of gender equality. This is a global problem, so it is essential to focus on changing the dynamic that has been going on for years. The figures show that the percentage of women in the technology industry is still quite low.


As can be seen from the graph, it is seen that there is no balance in terms of gender equality in the IT sector. The workforce of women in the IT sector is generally below 50%. These data are for 2021. Over time, the importance of gender equality in the it industry continues to increase. Equality in this area is expected to be achieved in the next 10-15 years.
